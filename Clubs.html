CLUBS

I am involved in SPIC MACAY, IIT Roorkee Chapter, onRec and QCG (Quantum Computing Group). In SPIC MACAY we conduct classical and folk events throughout the year and I have also been a co-coordinator for a workshop and event as well. We as volunteers ensure the smooth conduction of the event. This is very close to my heart since I like classical art and heritage of our country.

onRec is the official podcasting group of IIT Roorkee and we record podcasts with experts in various domains like Entrepreneurship, Consulting, ML, research along with several initiatives like First Impressions and Class of 2022 which captures the emotions of parents of entering students and emotions of students leaving the institute respectively.

QCG is a campus group quite actively involved in Quantum Computing and we have conducted hackathons in association with Amazon Bra Ket and IBM Qiskit. We also host open discussions every Saturday. I have delivered a talk in the series Many Body Quantum Theory meets Quantum Computation which was a collaboration between QCG and PaAC. I am currently an executive member of the club and was part of the organising team of Qiskit Fall Fest 2024.
